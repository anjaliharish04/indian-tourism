Explore Indian States

Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh, located in southeastern India, is known for its rich cultural heritage and the beautiful city of Hyderabad.

Arunachal Pradesh

Arunachal Pradesh is the northeastern state known for its picturesque landscapes, including lush forests and high-altitude lakes.

Assam

Assam, located in the northeastern part of India, is famous for its tea gardens, wildlife sanctuaries, and the Brahmaputra River.

Bihar

Bihar is home to ancient history, including Nalanda University, Bodh Gaya, and the ancient Mauryan Empire.

Chhattisgarh

Chhattisgarh, known for its tribal culture, lush forests, and ancient temples, is a land of natural beauty and history.

Goa

Goa, known for its pristine beaches and vibrant nightlife, is one of India's most popular tourist destinations.

Gujarat

Gujarat is known for its rich cultural heritage, the Rann of Kutch, and the Gir National Park, home to Asiatic lions.

Haryana

Haryana is known for its agricultural industry and being the birthplace of the Mahabharata, with historical sites like Kurukshetra.

Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh is known for its picturesque mountains, hill stations like Manali and Shimla, and adventure tourism.

Jharkhand

Jharkhand is known for its lush forests, wildlife, and being a hub for mining and steel industries.

Karnataka

Karnataka is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical sites like Hampi, and modern cities like Bangalore.

Kerala

Kerala, often called "God's Own Country," is known for its backwaters, hill stations, and pristine beaches.

Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh is known for its historical sites like Khajuraho, Sanchi, and the Kanha National Park.

Maharashtra

Maharashtra is known for its vibrant cities like Mumbai, historical caves like Elephanta, and rich cultural heritage.

Manipur

Manipur is known for its rich culture, classical dance forms, and scenic landscapes like Loktak Lake.

Meghalaya

Meghalaya is famous for its hill stations, waterfalls, and unique living root bridges in Cherrapunji.

Mizoram

Mizoram is known for its lush forests, tribal culture, and vibrant festivals like Chapchar Kut.

Nagaland

Nagaland is known for its rich tribal culture, scenic mountains, and vibrant festivals like Hornbill.

Odisha

Odisha is known for its ancient temples, vibrant culture, and the stunning Chilika Lake.

Punjab

Punjab is famous for its agriculture, vibrant Bhangra dance, and the Golden Temple.

Rajasthan

Rajasthan, the land of kings, is known for its majestic forts, palaces, and desert landscapes.

Sikkim, nestled in the Himalayas, is known for its monasteries and Kanchenjunga mountain views.

Tamil Nadu is famous for its classical dance, temple architecture, and vibrant festivals.

Telangana

Telangana is known for its historical landmarks, including Charminar and Golconda Fort.

Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh is famous for the Taj Mahal, Varanasi, and its rich cultural heritage.
